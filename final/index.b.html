BLONDE

 Frank Ocean’s latest album ‘Blonde’ gets me in the feels for no freaking reason. I think one of the songs that really makes me want to eat ice cream and cry is ‘Self-Control’. It’s just so sad. And beautiful. And catchy. And I think it’s the central song of this album. Note the verse: You cut your hair But you used to live a blonded life Blond is the title of the album. And “blonded” sounds awfully a lot like “blinded”. There’s that stupid saying that love is blind and something about people cut their hair after a break up.
